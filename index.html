1. 〜 proper*-Chise 〜

I work on continuous optimization. I studied numerical analysis up to my master’s degree, and was drawn by the analogies between the two fields — which led me to the world of continuous optimization.
My current research focuses on proposing algorithms via dynamical systems, with recent interests in discontinuous dynamics.
For more details, please see my research section in the bio.
